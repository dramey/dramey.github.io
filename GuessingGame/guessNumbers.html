Deb's Guessing Game

Welcome to my Guessing Game.

You will be able to enter one guess per text box. You will have 10 total tries to guess the sequence of three numbers.
If a number does not match the number that the box has, and does not match any of the numbers in the sequence, the box will be red!
If a number matches that number that the box has, the box will be green!
If a number does not match the number that the box has , but DOES match one of the numbers in the sequence, the box will be yellow!
