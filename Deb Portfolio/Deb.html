About

My name is Deborah Ramey. Welcome to my portfolio.

In September 2016, I started a program; In collaboration with the Tech Hire Eastern Kentucky (TEKY) program, I completed a 10-week accelerated boot camp. The 10-week program was split into two modules. The first being basic front-end web development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The second and longest phase of the program was spent learning iOS development, language, and principles. I learned the Swift programming language and wrote several apps to demonstrate my ability.

Some of those apps that I have created can be viewed by clicking the portfolio pictures above.

I also am very interested in Quality Assurance and App Design and would be passionate in developing the adequate skills to become an asset to any team!
