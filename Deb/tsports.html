Closer Look at my TEK eSports App!

The TEKY Sports App was a team collaboration app.

Our team wanted an App that users could use to track eSports tournaments

In the app the user has the ability to search for specific games

This app allows the user to see the flag of the country of the match and see start and end dates

There is a splash screen to the app, an about screen, and a feedback form that emails team members directly

The user can obtain news and videos and tell if a video is available prior to clicking
